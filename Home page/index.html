About the Workshop

Blockchain Technology is an emerging technology suitable for decentralised and transactional sharing of data across a large network of untrusted participants. It is an innovative distributed ledger technology where verification data for the transactional records is cryptographically secured against tampering and stored in blocks. This workshop is intended to provide opportunities for faculty, researchers and students to upgrade their knowledge in the area of Blockchain Technologies. Considering the growing importance of Blockchain Technologies and its potential in different sectors, this three day workshop is planned and sessions would be delivered by experts from academia and industry.

WORKSHOP OBJECTIVES

To disseminate the knowledge of Blockchain technology among students, researchers and academicians.

To discuss the current status of the Blockchain development and research work in India and across the world.
